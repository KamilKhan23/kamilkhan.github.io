About Me

I am a Computational Media researcher and game developer exploring how players form meaning, perception, and interpretation through interaction with procedural and AI-driven systems. My work lies at the intersection of game AI, expressive interaction, non-verbal systems, procedural generation, and AR perception pipelines.

I am currently completing a Bachelor's in Computer Systems Engineering at UET Peshawar (Expected 2026), where I have developed multiple technical and creative systems in Unity involving machine learning inference, procedural environments, and agent-based interaction.
