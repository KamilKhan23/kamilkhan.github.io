Writing Sample

Computational media fascinates me because meaning in games emerges from systems, not just text or narrative. A slight change in agent memory, timing, or gaze can alter how a player interprets intent. My research explores how non-verbal signals, procedural environments, and AI behaviors influence a player's understanding of a system, and how computational design can shape emotion, tension, and curiosity through interaction alone.

The projects above represent early steps in this direction — moving toward expressive AI systems that create interpretation rather than dictate it.
